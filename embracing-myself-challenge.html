HISTORY OF PRIDE MONTH

Since its beginnings, Pride Month has been about embracing identity and celebrating the freedom to be who you are.

Pride Month traces back to moments of courage and resistance that created space for visibility, equality, acceptance, and self-expression.

Over time, it has grown into a global reminder to practice acceptance, both from the world around us and within ourselves.

Today, Pride Month encourages each of us to reflect on who we are and begin to embrace every part of ourselves.

Why self-acceptance?

  • 🧡 Feel more at ease with yourself
  • 💙 Build a kinder inner voice
  • 💚 Reduce self-criticism
